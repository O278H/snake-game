## 🕹️ How to Play  
- Press **Spacebar** to start the game.  
- Use the **Arrow Keys** ⬆️⬇️⬅️➡️ to control the snake.  
- Eat the food 🍎 to grow and increase your score.  
- Don’t crash into the walls or your own tail ❌.
